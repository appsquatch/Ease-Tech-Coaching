A little bit about us

Ease is an Austin, Texas startup created by two former technical support advisors in the mobile industry. In our line of work, we spoke with many people who had trouble finding someone to help them understand and troubleshoot all of the increasingly complex ways their devices and services interconnected.

Many seemed to often get the run-around when contacting technical support for various services and devices that worked together but were made by different companies.

Ease was started to solve this problem. We provides one-on-one technology coaching as a means to teach people to use their technology the way they want. At the same time, we provide tech savvy individuals with meaningful work that utilizes their technical ability.

How it Works

What we help with

We can help with a wide variety of tech devices such as Smartphones, computers, laptops, Smart TV's, Smart Home Devices, Smart Speakers, Bluetooth Devices, Game consoles, Printers and so on.


We want you to get the most out of the session so we tailor each appointment to your needs. To give you a few examples of common requests; New Device Setup, Fixing annoying error messages, How to add music to your phone, etc.
